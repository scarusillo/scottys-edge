Model Changelog

How the Model Has Evolved

Every substantive change to the model, published with its version, date, and rationale. The full git history is publicly readable at github.com/scarusillo/scottys-edge — this page is the curated summary of the changes that mattered.

If you see a change that moved numbers against us being honestly documented here, that's the point. If we ever silently changed a rule to make a past loss look better, we'd have to hide that commit. We don't.

v25.39
Apr 20, 2026
Live

Context Model live (NHL + MLS + EPL spreads)

New module (context_model.py) running alongside the Elo-based model. Layers 13 real-world signals — injuries, last-5 and last-20 form, rest/back-to-back, tanking motivation, playoff home-court bonus, playoff injury amplification (shrunk rotations), series momentum, team-specific home/away splits, H2H regular-season margin, pace/scoring differential, star concentration amplifier — onto the raw Elo spread. When Elo diverges from market but the context-adjusted projection falls back within the divergence threshold, fires a DATA_SPREAD pick on Context's preferred side at the market line, 5u stake, ≤+140 odds.

Why: Elo knows team strength but nothing about tonight — who's hurt, who's coasting, how these teams match up. On NHL/MLS/EPL, 13 signals close the gap. 14-day backtest: NHL 11-3 (78.6%, +35.00u), MLS 5-0 (+22.73u), EPL 2-0 (+9.09u). NBA excluded — Elo errors on playoff spreads (10+ pts) are structurally too large for adjustments (1-5 pts) to close.

SPREAD_FADE_FLIP (NBA + NHL)

When DIVERGENCE_GATE blocks a spread pick on NBA or NHL, we now bet the OPPOSITE side of what the Elo model wanted — market line, market odds, 5u stake. Backtest on 14 days of blocked picks: NBA 38-18 (67.9%, +82.73u), NHL 17-4 (81.0%, +57.27u). Combined +140u. Context Model takes precedence on NHL when it unblocks; fade flip is the fallback.

Why: Our Elo spread model is wrong ~70% of the time on big divergences — especially playoff/injury situations. Fading is contrarian-to-our-own-model and works precisely because the model is broken for that regime. It's a bandage, not a fix; real fix is a better spread model (Context Model is step 1 of that).

SHARP_OPPOSES_BLOCK gate (NHL + NCAA Baseball)

Blocks game-line picks on these two sports when the opener-to-current line moved against our side past the steam-engine threshold. Post-Apr-1 backtest: 22 picks saved, +24.91u.

Why: NHL 8-9 (-14.26u) and NCAA Baseball 1-3-1 (-10.65u) on SHARP_OPPOSES picks — the market moved against us and we lost. MLB blocked at 50% hit rate (juice drag, not signal) — no structural lever.

NBA PRA book-arb — SHADOW mode

Points+Rebounds+Assists prop collection enabled in the API fetch. Arb scanner detects sharp-vs-soft line gaps ≥ 2.5 and logs to shadow_blocked_picks with reason PROP_BOOK_ARB_SHADOW — no live bet. Promote to live betting at n=15 candidates with ≥55% counterfactual W/L.

Why: Zero historical PRA snapshots (never requested before). Combo props have different volatility math than singles — threshold 2.5 is an educated guess. Shadow-first protects the bankroll while we accumulate data.

Concentration cap bug fix

Today's first SPREAD_FADE_FLIP on DEN/MIN was blocked because a misrouted Ayo Dosunmu prop shared an Odds API event_id with the game. The cap logic was asymmetric — new PROPs were exempt from being capped, but EXISTING props were still populating the event-watchlist that blocks game-line picks. Fixed: props no longer populate the cap list.

Why: Caught live by the fade-flip rollout. Props are independent bets from game-line picks; they shouldn't block each other.

SQL injection patches + data-model docs

Parameterized sport_filter queries in backtest_prop_model.py and backtest_props.py (was string-interpolated). Added explicit documentation in grader.py + CLAUDE.md that graded_bets is authoritative for performance analysis — TAINTED/DUPLICATE rows in bets are intentionally excluded, and backfilled grades may have bet_id IS NULL.

Why: Code audit findings from the morning. No observed exploit, but the patch is cheap and the docs prevent future analysis errors.

Opener capture scheduled at 5:30 AM ET

New Windows scheduled task ScottysEdge_Opener_530AM runs main.py opener 30 min before the 6 AM hourly pipeline. Catch-up-on-login enabled — if the PC is off on weekends, the task runs as soon as the PC powers on.

Why: Before this, the 6 AM hourly run was the FIRST to see today's lines, so opener = current snapshot. Steam/divergence gates couldn't detect any movement on 6 AM picks. Now we have a 30-min window for real line movement to be visible.

v25.34
Apr 19, 2026
Live

PROP_BOOK_ARB unblocked

Cross-book prop line disagreement scanner was detecting signals for 2 days without firing any picks — the downstream filter required 20% edge + ELITE confidence, which arb picks never meet by design. Whitelisted BOOK_ARB and PROP_BOOK_ARB side types to bypass the filter; they have their own thresholds at the scanner.

Why: Backfilled 3 picks from Apr 18 (Murray OVER 23.5, Walter OVER 6.5, Randle OVER 18.5) for honest record accounting — 2W-1L, +3.17u.

Prop arb thresholds tightened

Per-stat minimum gaps raised ~1.5×: points 1.5 → 2.0, assists 1.0 → 1.5, rebounds 1.0 → 1.5, threes/blocks/steals/SOG 0.5 → 1.0, pitcher Ks 1.0 → 1.5.

Why: First-day sim showed 1.5+ gap went 2W-1L while 1.0 gap went 1W-4L. Tight enough to block the noise, wide enough to keep the real signals.

SCRUB safety net in grader

If a bet's context contains a SCRUB: tag at grade time, the result is forced to TAINTED regardless of the game outcome. Added cmd_unscrub helper that properly reverses scrubs (strips the tag, clears result, deletes TAINTED graded row).

Why: Miami/Stanford bet had lingering SCRUB tag after manual unscrub and was counted as a WIN. The record should never contradict the context.

prop_snapshots archival

Split the 6.1M-row prop_snapshots table into a 696K-row live table (last 7 days) + 5.4M-row archive. Scanner queries 3.3× faster. Full history preserved via a UNION VIEW for backtests.

Why: Runs were trending 15-20 min. Database bloat was the biggest factor.

Parallel API fetching

Steps 3 (odds), 4 (props), and 4c (referees) now fetch all sports concurrently via ThreadPoolExecutor (max_workers=5). Network I/O overlapped; DB writes serialize.

Why: 14 sports × 2s API calls = 28s serial. Parallelized reduces to ~6s for the same work.

SCORES step skipped on hourly runs

Step 1 (score fetching) removed from hourly runs — scores are only needed for grading, which cmd_grade runs independently at 4am.

Why: Saves ~30-60s per hourly run on 14 redundant API calls.

v25.32
Apr 18, 2026
Live

NCAA_ERA_RELIABILITY_GATE

For NCAA baseball totals, if either starter has <15 career IP, the pick is blocked. Starter ERA on 1-inning samples is noise, not signal.

Why: Vanderbilt's Stillman at 27.00 ERA over 0.2 IP triggered a false "weak pitching, bet OVER" edge. Thin-IP starters are the single most common source of bad prop and total edges.

Doubleheader exclusion

When two games between the same teams fall on the same day (MLB/NCAA doubleheaders), the scraper now matches starters to the correct game explicitly, not by first-match heuristic.

Why: Miami/Stanford game 2 was being priced with game 1's starters from a pre-game scrape.

v25.31
Apr 17, 2026
Live

PROP_FADE_FLIP mechanism

When our own prop model projects a stat ≥3.0 points different from the market median (on a 4+ book consensus), we flip direction — fade the model, bet with the market. Sized at 5u (max play).

Why: Backtest showed wide model-market gaps correlate with market-right outcomes in playoff/late-season NBA contexts. The mechanism protects us from rate-model miscalibrations.

PROP_BOOK_ARB scanner added

New scanner for cross-book prop disagreement: when sharp books (FD, BR) and soft books (DK, BetMGM, Caesars, Fanatics, ESPN BET) post meaningfully different lines on the same player + stat, we bet the soft side at the soft book.

Why: Same thesis as game-line book-arb (v25.28) — soft books lag in adjusting prop lines. Scanner was deployed, then sat dormant for 2 days due to the v25.34 filter bug.

PROP_DIVERGENCE_GATE

New gate that blocks prop picks where the model projection diverges from the market median by more than a per-stat threshold.

Why: The Kennard pick trigger — model projected a radically different stat than consensus without enough signal to justify it.

v25.28–29
Apr 17, 2026
Live

Book-arb expanded to NBA totals + NHL/MLB spreads

Added book-arb gates for NBA totals (sharp/soft gap ≥ 1.0), NHL spreads (gap ≥ 1.5), MLB spreads (gap ≥ 1.5). Sized at 5u each.

Why: NCAA baseball book-arb (v25.25) validated the mechanism. Multi-sport expansion multiplies the signal.

NHL spread divergence threshold loosened 1.5 → 2.5 (v25.29)

The old 1.5-point threshold was flagging too many normal games. Raised to 2.5. Picks that would have fired at 1.5-2.5 are now tagged DIV_EXPANDED for monitoring but not bet.

Why: Calibration showed 1.5-2.5 range was mostly noise. Real divergence signal appears at 2.5+.

v25.22–27
Apr 17, 2026
Live

NCAA DraftKings gate overhaul

Five new gates targeting a specific DK routing failure on NCAA baseball totals: NCAA_DK_TIGHT_SKIP (skip when all books at same line — market efficient), NCAA_DK_FADE_FLIP (flip to opposite side when DK soft vs sharp opener), NCAA_DK_SHARP_VETO, NCAA_NO_SHARP_SKIP (skip when neither FD nor BR posted opener), NCAA_SHARP_LINE_MOVE (sharp line moved significantly post-opener). Also NCAA_DK_FLIP_BLOCKED (v25.27) prevents flipping when a sharp book agrees with our original model side.

Why: Backtest showed DK was routing us into NCAA totals that went 1W-6L. Isolating the bad signatures and either blocking or flipping them recovered +47u on backtest. Live: DK NCAA totals have gone from -25.6u pre-overhaul to 0 bets post-overhaul.

NCAA baseball book-arb (v25.25)

FanDuel vs DraftKings opener gap ≥ 2.0 points triggers a book-arb pick. Sized at 5u.

v25.13
Apr 10, 2026
Live

MAX_PROP_ODDS lowered +150 → +140

No player prop will fire at any book if the odds are above +140. Additional guard: if ANY book offering the same prop has odds above +150, the pick is killed across all books.

Why: Calibration showed +141 to +150 bucket went 1W-6L — rate-based projections systematically overconfident on rare-event props. See the errors page for the full post-mortem.

v22 NCAA baseball UNDER filters removed

Rolled back the v22 "block all Friday NCAA UNDERs" and "block NCAA UNDERs at line > 12.0" gates. These were blocking ~140u/month of winners.

Prop model overhaul

Rebuilt the prop projection engine: moved from Poisson modeling to empirical hit-rate estimation on rolling 20-game + season samples. Added cross-book consensus engine. Capped max prop odds at +150 (later tightened to +140). Added a timing gate: MLB props only fire within a 3-hour pre-game window. Batting order tracking for MLB counting-stat props.

Why: Props had been the model's weakest point. The overhaul was the largest single change since the late-March rebuild cutoff.

v21 — raised edge floors to 20%, shadowed losing context factors

The "post-rebuild" cutoff. All edge floors raised to 20%. Six context factors that were losing (Home fast-paced, Away bounce-back, Altitude, Home hot streak, Away revenge, Away letdown) were turned off (shadowed) while still tracked.
